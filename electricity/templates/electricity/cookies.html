01.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They help websites function, remember your preferences, and provide usage insights so services can be improved.

02.

Why RWM EL Uses Cookies

We use cookies to ensure our digital platforms operate reliably, protect customer data, and deliver a tailored experience during service requests and quote configurations.

Core Functionality

Secure login, request tracking, and session management during service workflows.

Security Monitoring

Protecting user data and preventing unauthorized access to client information.

03.

Types of Cookies We Use

Functional

Preferences such as language selection and localized electrical standards.

Understanding site traffic to improve performance and usability.

Marketing

Showing relevant advertising on other sites based on your interests.
